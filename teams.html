F1 Teams

Constructors and Competition

Teams, also called "constructors" in F1, design and build the cars according to strict FIA regulations. Ferrari, McLaren, and Mercedes are among the most iconic in the sport.

Ferrari
Founded in 1947, Scuderia Ferrari is the oldest active F1 team.
McLaren
Based in the UK, known for producing both F1 cars and road cars.
Mercedes
Dominated the hybrid era, winning several consecutive world titles.
