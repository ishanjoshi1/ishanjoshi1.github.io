A Brief History of Formula 1

How It All Started

The first Formula 1 World Championship was held in 1950. Over the decades, F1 evolved to become the pinnacle of motorsport technology and driver skill.

The 1950s

Juan Manuel Fangio dominated the early years with 5 world titles.

The Modern Era

Technology has advanced significantly, with hybrids and turbo engines showcasing innovation in racing.
