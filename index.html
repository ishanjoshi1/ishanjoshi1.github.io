Welcome to the World of Formula 1

The Pinnacle of Motorsports

Formula 1 (F1) is the highest class of international auto racing for open-wheel single-seater formula racing cars. Organized by the FIA, the series is owned by the Formula One Group.

This website will give you an overview of some of the most legendary drivers, iconic teams, and historic circuits in the world of F1.

Learn more about current standings on the Official Formula 1 website.
